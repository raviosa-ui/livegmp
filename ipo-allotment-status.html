How to Check IPO Allotment Status

IPO allotment is finalised by the registrar of the issue, usually within a day or two of the IPO closing. You can check your allotment on the registrar's website using your PAN, application number or DP/Client ID.

Registrar links

MUFG Intime (Link Intime)Check allotment on Link Intime
KFin Technologies (KFintech)Check allotment on KFintech

Registrar websites occasionally change their URLs around big IPOs. If a link doesn't open, search the registrar's name + "IPO allotment status".

Check via the exchanges

You can also verify allotment on BSE's application status page (select "Equity", choose the IPO, enter application number and PAN) or on NSE's IPO application verification page after logging in with your PAN.

Typical timeline

IPO closesDay T
Allotment finalisedT+1 (usually)
Refunds initiated / funds unblockedT+1 to T+2
Shares credited to dematT+2
Listing dayT+3

Didn't get allotment?

Oversubscribed retail portions are allotted by lottery — one lot per selected applicant. Blocked funds are automatically released via your UPI/ASBA mandate. Track how the stock actually lists against expectations on our live GMP page.
