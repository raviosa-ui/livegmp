What is IPO Grey Market Premium (GMP)?

IPO GMP is the premium at which IPO shares change hands in the unofficial grey market before they list on the stock exchange. If an IPO priced at ₹100 has a GMP of ₹30, the market expects it to list around ₹130. GMP moves with subscription demand and overall market sentiment, and can change several times a day.

GMP is not an official or regulated number — treat it as a sentiment indicator, not a guarantee. Read our full guide: What is GMP and how is it decided?
