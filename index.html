What is GMP?

The IPO Grey Market Premium (GMP) is an unofficial price at which an IPO is traded before listing on stock exchanges.

• IPO Price = Kostak rate (fixed amount per application)
• Listing Gain = Expected profit/loss percentage on listing day (Subject to Sauda)
